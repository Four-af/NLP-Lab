Kusuriya no Hitorigoto
?

is a Japanese media franchise that started with a web novel written by
Natsu Hyūga
and illustrated by
Touko Shino
. It was
serialized online
since 2011 on the user generated novel publishing website
Shōsetsuka ni Narō
. In the following year it was acquired by
Shufunotomo
who initially published the series as a
novel
with a single volume in 2012 and then in 2014 began to publish it yet again this time as a multi volume
light novel
. Later in 2017 two manga adaptations started serializing
The Apothecary Diaries
in
Monthly Big Gangan
from spring of that year and then
The Apothecary Diaries Maomao s Rear Palace Mystery Solving Notebook
in
Sunday Gene X
from late summer.

Plot


There is a certain large country in the central plains of the mainland. In the imperial court of its emperor there is a girl. Her name is
Maomao
a pharmacist from the pleasure district currently working as a maidservant in the inner palace. The girl surely not considered a beauty is keeping a low profile waiting for her contract to end. She was confident that she would not be \ visited by the emperor. During that she learned about the short lives of the children of the emperor.
Maomao
hearing that the two surviving children are critically ill begins her investigation for the cause .
Set in the middle ages of the East the “Food Taster” girl continues to solve difficult cases in the imperial court one after another.
